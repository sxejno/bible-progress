What Makes This Different?

Word-Weighted Tracking

Progress is tracked by word count, not just chapters. Psalm 117 has 33 words. Psalm 119 has 2,423 words.

Who Made This?

Made by a friend!

I noticed that the Bible reading progress trackers I found counted chapters as equal slices, when that is not really the case with the word counts or time they take to read, so here is a simple tracker that reflects actual reading effort!
